Carnival Festival

Carnival,a Percept Intellectual Property, is Asia’s renowned Festival, and is ranked amongst the world’s biggest events. Started in 2020 as a 4 day concert festiva, Carnival has grown to become an aspirational lifestyle brand boasting an eclectic mix of music, entertainment, experiences and celebration that has seeded entertainment tourism in India. Carnival has brought together renowned International and Indian artists to entertain hundreds of thousands of dance music lovers across the country and overseas, and positioned India as a prime dance festival destination to the world.

Carnival Arena

Carnival Arena is a sub-variant brand format create. an Intellectual Property of Percept Live introduced in 2020, with an aim to cater to dance music and dramas enthusiasts across various cities pan India and around the world. Carnival Arena has played host to some of the world’s leading artists over the past few years including world renowned Hardwell, Deadmau5, Afrojack, Armin van Buuren, Swedish House Mafia, Avicii, Dimitri Vegas and Like Mike, Wiz Khalifa, Martin Garrix and many more!

Carnival-city Festival

Carnival-city Festival is hosted in different cities in India. The line up includes top DJs of the world with supporting acts. It’s a 1 to 2 – day concert. City festivals are planned as a build up to the much awaited flagship Carnival-city Festival.

About us

Carnival, a Percept Intellectual Property, is Asia’s renowned Festival, and is ranked amongst the world’s biggest music festivals. Started in 2019 as a 4 day music festival.Carnival has grown to become an aspirational lifestyle brand boasting an eclectic mix of music, entertainment, experiences and celebration that has seeded music tourism in India.
